What Extensive Vehicle Checks Are Performed?

When you book a Pit Stop Extensive Ezy Service package with Pit Stop, a experienced vehicle technician will carry out the work on your car. Here are some of the checks that you can expect with the Extensive Ezy Service.

  • Change the oil with up to 4.5 litres of 5w30 grade oil.
  • Replace oil filter.
  • Check and top up all essential fluids e.g. clutch, brake & power steering fluid.
  • Check and top up washer and engine coolant levels
  • Check cambelt due date. Check battery.
  • Check air filter.
  • Check CV boots & joints.
  • Check brake pads, rotors & drums.
  • Computer & systems health check.
  • Lubricate door hinges.
  • Road test & report.

Extensive Service Package Key Benefits

The Extensive Ezy service package is designed to help prolong your vehicle's life, safety, and performance. It includes up to 4.5 litres of 5W30 grade oil, a standard oil filter, and essential fluid top-ups to keep your engine running smoothly.

A comprehensive 25-point service check and a full computer and system health check ensure your vehicle is thoroughly inspected and maintained. All work is carried out by experienced Pit Stop technicians, giving you peace of mind and professional care you can trust.
