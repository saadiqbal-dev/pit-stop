New Zealand's Trusted Name in Car Servicing and Automotive Repairs.

Pit Stop has been proudly New Zealand owned and operated for over 45 years, growing into a trusted homegrown institution with more than 40 stores nationwide. From vehicle servicing and repairs to WOFs, brakes, exhausts, shock absorbers, and tyres — we've got all your car service needs covered. As New Zealand's largest franchise chain of automotive service stores, you can count on your local Pit Stop to keep you moving.
